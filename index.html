ABOUT ZEGE MOTORS

Who we are?

Mr. Sridhar’s Zegemotors is a one-of-a-kind automobile company dedicated to making each vehicle luxurious. Mr.Sridhar and his employees endeavor towards servicing, repairing, and maintaining pre-owned autos. We’ve already served about 750 happy customers and expect to serve many more in the future. In addition, we’ve bought and sold 60 pre-owned autos. To achieve the title of “A Luxury Car Specialist,” We provide solutions to all automobile problems.
